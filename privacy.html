Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Expedition Auto is a Chrome extension that automates the completion of videos and questionnaires on the Expedition EY platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension does and does not collect.

Data We Do Not Collect

Expedition Auto does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, ID)
  • Login credentials or session tokens
  • Browsing history or activity outside of Expedition EY
  • Any data to our own servers (we have no backend)

How the Extension Works

When you click the button in the extension popup, the extension runs JavaScript directly in the context of the Expedition EY tab you already have open. It uses your existing browser session (cookies already set by the platform) to make API requests to the Expedition EY platform on your behalf.

All communication is exclusively between your browser and the Expedition EY platform (us-webapp.spotme.com). No data passes through any third-party server.

Permissions Used

  • activeTab: identifies the current Expedition EY tab and injects the automation script into it.
  • scripting: executes the completion logic within the Expedition EY page context, where your session cookies are already available.

These permissions are the minimum required for the extension to function. No host permissions are requested beyond what is needed to operate on the Expedition EY domain.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page.
