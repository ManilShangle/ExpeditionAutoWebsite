Support & FAQ

Common questions about Expedition Auto.

The extension says "Navigate to Expedition EY first" — what do I do?

Make sure you're logged in and on a page at us-webapp.spotme.com. The extension only works on that domain. Open any Expedition EY page, then click the extension button again.

Some items show ✗ in the log — why?

Occasionally the platform API returns an error for a specific activity. This can happen if the activity has special requirements or has already been completed. The extension will skip it and continue with the rest.

Will this work if I've already completed some activities?

Yes. The extension checks the completion status of every activity first and only processes the ones that are still incomplete. Already-completed items are skipped automatically.

Is my data safe? Does the extension send anything to a server?

No. The extension runs entirely in your browser using your existing session. No data is sent to any third-party server. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Live event attendance still shows as incomplete — can the extension complete those?

No. Live event attendance is awarded by EY administrators after you actually attend the live session. This cannot be automated, and the extension skips those activities intentionally.

The total points didn't update immediately — is that normal?

Yes. The platform may take a few seconds to process all the completions. Refresh the Expedition EY page and your updated point total will appear.
